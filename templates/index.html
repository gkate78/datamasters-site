DataMasters

DataMasters is a community-driven initiative by Data Engineering Pilipinas (DEP), a PyData Group founded by Mr. Myk Ogbinar. It brings together a dedicated group of individuals passionate about honing their project presentation, interview skills, and knowledge sharing in a fully supportive and collaborative environment.

History

The DataMasters & Presenters Online Program was officially initiated by Ms. Sandy Cabanes, an active member, community leader, and moderator of the Data Engineering Pilipinas community. She plays a key role in organizing online presentations and webinars to foster knowledge sharing, demonstrating a strong commitment to advancing data engineering practices and supporting the professional growth of her peers.

Mission

Provide a fully supportive environment for aspiring data professionals to refine their project presentation and interview skills.

Vision

Establish DataMasters as a leading community program where data professionals can master the art of presenting their work clearly and confidently and build interview skills. We envision a space where participants feel empowered to share their ideas, receive constructive feedback, and inspire others to excel in their professional and personal journeys.

Core Values

  • Inclusivity
  • Collaboration
  • Continuous Improvement
  • Confidence-Building
  • Celebration of Progress

What We Offer

  • Safe Practice Environment
  • Constructive Feedback
  • Peer Networking
  • Recognition
  • Skill Development Workshops
